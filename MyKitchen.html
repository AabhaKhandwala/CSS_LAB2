ROASTED VEGGIE QUESADILLA

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium Onion (chopped)
  • 1 medium Green Pepper (chopped)
  • 1 medium Zucchini (chopped)
  • 1/2 medium Red Pepper (chopped)
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon blackpepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 6 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup Guacamole

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F. On the baking sheet toss all veggies with olive oil. sprinkle salt , pepper and taco seasoning. Bake 15 to 20 minutes till veggies get tender.
  2. After baking toss the mixer.
  3. Roast the tortilla both side on pan.
  4. Top with veggie mix and cheese.
  5. Cover with another tortilla and roast both sides till golden brown with butter.
  6. Cut into quarters and transfer quesadillas to plates. Serve with lettuce, salsa, guacamole and sour cream on the side.
